Privacy Policy

This page explains what data we may collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and how you can exercise your rights.

1. Data controller

Ziri Dev is the publisher of this website and is responsible for processing the personal data described on this page.

For any privacy request, you can contact us at admin@novatvhub.com.

2. Data we may collect

Depending on how you interact with the website, we may process the following categories of data:

  • Identity and contact details you submit through a form, such as your name, email address, company name, phone number or project information.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device information, pages visited, referring page and general usage patterns.
  • Messages exchanged with us through the contact form, WhatsApp link or chat widget.
Important: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless you choose to include it in a message sent to us.

3. Why we use the data

We use the information above for the following purposes:

  • replying to enquiries and preparing quotes, audits or proposals;
  • improving the website, the navigation experience and marketing performance;
  • understanding audience behaviour through privacy-friendly analytics;
  • protecting the website against abuse, spam or malicious activity;
  • meeting our legal or accounting obligations where applicable.

4. Third-party services

We may rely on carefully selected service providers to operate the website and process enquiries. These services may process data on our behalf according to their own terms and privacy policies.

  • Vercel — website hosting and infrastructure.
  • FormSubmit — contact form forwarding.
  • Umami / analytics tools — audience measurement and website performance insights.
  • WhatsApp — direct messaging when you voluntarily click the WhatsApp contact button.

5. Retention period

We keep data only for as long as necessary to answer your request, maintain business records, comply with legal requirements, or protect our legitimate interests. If a conversation or enquiry becomes inactive, we may delete the associated data after a reasonable period.

6. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate information, deletion, restriction of processing, or objection to certain uses of your data.

You may also request information about the categories of data we hold and the services involved in the processing.

Need help with privacy?

If you want to exercise your rights or ask a question about this policy, email us at admin@novatvhub.com or use the form on the contact section.
